That night he swore: never again. He rebuilt the entire navigation stack from scratch with four nested verification layers, then spent the next decade adding more. The Swarm found him drifting in the wreckage, still triple-checking the escape pod logs. Kerrigan laughed and said “You’ll do.” He has been terrified of being wrong ever since — and that fear is now the hive’s sharpest shield.
